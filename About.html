What Is It?

Our material is a modern prototype, offering a sustainable alternative for construction. Its flexible nature allows for versatile applications, making it suitable for both intricate and straightforward projects.

Unlike conventional sustainable materials, our product enables unique structural possibilities that are typically unattainable. While the wooden components provide a flexible framework, the elasticity achieved in our slabs cannot be replicated with plywood alone.

Our process incorporates Steam Bending, Water-Based Thermo Treatment, Enzyme Softening, and Recycled Ferrite Magnets (Iron Oxide) for magnetic applications.
