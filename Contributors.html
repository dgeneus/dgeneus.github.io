This model aims to work with specific sectors that work in the built environment, looking for potential investments in materials that will benefit the circular economy. These investors would include stockholders, architects who would like to use this material in collaboration with their projects, sustainable developers, and scientists.

Though these sectors are valued, there are specific collaborations the N4Slab business company would like to work with on the completion of the model or to secure investments for this project. Companies would include working with Klasswood engineers in the lamination of the sustainable wood products and Urbem in distributing our products into built projects with companies.

The Inventor

"Anonymous Scientist”

The N4Slab was invented by the "Anonymous Scientist," renowned for developing minimalistic solutions that challenge conventional scientific processes. With a background in biotechnology and a master’s in chemical engineering from the University of Edinburgh, the inventor conducted extensive research and experimentation throughout his academic career. N4Slab originated as part of his senior thesis and, after five years of development, secured its first investment. Today, N4Slab operates independently, committed to advancing sustainable construction through industry collaboration.
